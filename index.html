About Us

Prodigy Infotech is a leading provider of coding and technology education. Our mission is to empower individuals with the skills they need to thrive in the digital world. We offer a range of courses designed to help beginners and experienced coders alike. Our experienced instructors, industry-aligned curriculum, and hands-on approach ensure that our students are well-prepared for real-world challenges.

At Prodigy Infotech, we believe that anyone can learn to code, and we are committed to making coding education accessible and engaging. Join us to start your journey in the exciting world of technology.

Our Courses

Web Development

Build responsive and modern websites. This course covers HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks like React and Angular. You'll learn how to create visually appealing and functional websites from scratch.

Data Science

Analyze data and gain insights. This course covers Python, R, SQL, and various data visualization tools. You'll learn how to handle large datasets, perform statistical analyses, and present your findings effectively.

Machine Learning

Explore the world of AI and machine learning. This course covers supervised and unsupervised learning, neural networks, and deep learning. You'll gain hands-on experience with popular ML frameworks like TensorFlow and PyTorch.

Cyber Security

Protect systems, networks, and programs from digital attacks. This course covers network security, cryptography, risk management, and ethical hacking. You'll learn how to secure sensitive information and defend against cyber threats.

Mobile Development

Create mobile applications for Android and iOS. This course covers Java, Swift, and cross-platform development tools like Flutter and React Native. You'll learn how to design, develop, and deploy mobile apps.

Cloud Computing

Learn to design, deploy, and manage cloud-based applications. This course covers AWS, Azure, Google Cloud, and cloud architecture. You'll gain skills in cloud infrastructure, services, and security.
